Join the Guild

Civic Guild is in formation, and we are looking for founding members who want to shape its direction.

Who Can Join

Membership is open to anyone who works in a technical or policy role in technology companies — in Silicon Valley or the broader U.S. tech ecosystem — and commits to our principles. Members participate as individuals, not as representatives of their employers.

What You Sign Up For

Members commit to a shared set of foundational principles:

  • Democratic governance and the rule of law are essential for transformative technologies to benefit humanity.
  • Rigorous scientific inquiry is essential for transformative technologies to benefit humanity.
  • Open societies that welcome talent and diverse perspectives are essential for transformative technologies to benefit humanity.
  • International cooperation between democracies is essential for transformative technologies to benefit humanity.

Read the full principles →

What Members Do

Members participate in a trusted community of practitioners, lend technical talent to democracy-strengthening organizations, and engage in policy processes grounded in technical rigor. We convene through gatherings, working groups, and private forums.
